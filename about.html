Our Story

It all began with a fan poll ... that went viral.
Bored at home and starved of NBA action , we posted a 2019-20 MVP Poll on Reddit.
Very simple, the respondents got 2 picks from 15 choices and an 'Other:' option.
It got over 2,500 responses within 16 hours of posting.
Following it's success and after observing the discussions in the comments,
we decided to start a basketball polls newsletter.
A new edition is sent every Sunday, it contains the previous edition's results and new polls.
